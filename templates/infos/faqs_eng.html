F.A.Q.

What is this service for?

Trip&Sale is created for people who would like to buy products and wares at their “world prices”, without overpayment through the currency conversion or taxes. Besides You can acquire exclusive souvenirs from any foreign country (Russian doll – Matryoshka, or Blue Thai Tea, for example). Surprise your friends and family with a rare and unusual gift!

How can I, living, for example, in the USA, get a product from another country without overpaying?

You can get it with the help of the travelers and tourists from your country who can buy the product in another country during their journey and then deliver it to you.

How Trip&Sale can help me to buy something at a lower price?

Our service is a sort of facilitator between the travelers, who register their routs, and the customers, who draw up the requirements for the particular things.

Is it free?

The service is free.

The price depends only on an agreement between the traveler and the customer. It could be the pure price of the product, but, please, remember, that the travelers would be pleased to have a reward for their effort.

If the deal is straight between the traveler and the customer, the payment is made any way the parties will agree on.

The only paid option is «Guarantee»-service. This service is not obligatory for making a deal and could be chosen at customer’s discretion.

«Guarantee»-service

What is it for?

This service, provided by Trip&Sale, insures our clients and their deals.

If You chose this option, we will mediate your deal and insuring it against of any kind of accidental losses or emergencies.

In what cases is it worth using this service?

  • You are a traveler and You do not trust the customer

    The customer asked You to buy an expensive product. You have already bought it, but the customer canceled the deal in the very last moment. Now You have an expensive Something You don’t need. If You have made the deal with the Guarantee”- option, the whole sum of money You have mentioned in the arrangement will be given to You in any case if You fulfilled the terms of this arrangement.

  • You are a customer and You do not trust the traveler

    The traveler purposely brake the terms of the agreement and brought a damaged product. If You have made the deal with the «Guarantee«- option, You can be sure, that You won’t pay if the executor deliberately defied the agreement or did not notify You that he or she failed to find the product.

What should I do to make a deal with this «Guarantee»-service?

  1. The customer and the traveler agree on the price (the real price + the reward for the traveler), the meeting place and the terms via the dialogue-window on our website tripnsale.com.
  2. In the dialogue-window there is a button “Make a deal with the «Guarantee»-service”. When both the customer and the traveler click on this button, the conference-dialogue between the customer, the traveler and our Agent is appeared.
  3. The customer and the traveler tell the Agent all the details of the deal (the price, the place and the terms).
  4. The customer transmits the agreed sum of money to the Agent’s account. Thereafter the deal is regarded as «in process», and the traveler can have his money back only if the traveler breaks the terms of the deal.
  5. The Agent acknowledges the receipt of money. Thereafter the traveler can be sure that he or she will receive the agreed sum of money even if the customer cancels the order.
  6. After the customer gets his order from the traveler, both of them should point in the dialogue that the deal is executed and they have no claims towards each other. We highly recommend the parties to do when the meet.
  7. After both the parties certified the accomplishment of the deal, the Agent transmits the agreed sum of money to the travelers account.

What exactly does the Agent do in a disputable situation?

  1. The «Guarantee»-service transmits the whole sum of money to the traveler’s account, if the deal was marked as being «in process», but the customer canceled the order.
  2. The «Guarantee»-service transmits the whole sum of money to the customer’s account, if the deal was marked as being «in process», but the traveler broke the terms of the deal or canceled it.
  3. The Agent reserves the right to solve the disputable situations with the assistance of both the parties.

How much does this service cost?

The service takes 2% of the deal’s price.

How long does it take to deliver my order?

It depends directly on the time the traveler that You choose will spend in his or her journey and your agreement with the traveler.

The traveler damaged or broke the product. What can be done?

If You made a deal using the «Guarantee»-option, we will return You the whole sum of money You have paid, if the product was damaged through the traveler’s fault.

If a deal was made without the «Guarantee»-option, the users solve all kinds of disputable situations themselves.

I have already bought the product, but the customer unexpectedly canceled his order.

If You made a deal using the «Guarantee»-option and the deal was marked as «in process» (it means that the customer has already transmitted money to the Agent), we will return You the whole sum of money You have spend.

Trip&Sale strongly recommends the travelers to buy the product after the deal is marked as being «in process». The Agent will notify the both parties about it.
